The Practice is easily accessible from most Central and Greater London locations either via tube, rail or road. 

Parking

There is Free On-Street Parking

By Car

The practice is conveniently located just off the main Watford Road and East Lane, not far from Play Golf Northwick Park and The Clementine Churchill Hospital.

It is approx. a 10-15 minute drive from the North Circular Road, Alperton and the A40 Western Avenue, Greenford. 

Further Details

For further details on how to reach the practice by road from your location, enter HA0 3JL in your navigation application or visit route planning sites such as:
Google Maps, AA Route Planner, RAC Route Planner, Green Flag Route Planner

Public Transport

The area is well serviced by public transport. Below is some information to assist you in planning your journey.

Bus Routes

Bus Routes 483, 182, N18 245 and 92 all bring you within 3 - 15 minute walking distance from the practice.

Underground Routes

Harrow-on-the-Hill (Metropolitan Line), Sudbury Town Station (Piccadilly Line), Sudbury Hill Station (Piccadilly Line), Sudbury Hill Harrow (Chiltern Overground), Sudbury & Harrow Road (Chiltern Overground), South Kenton Station (Bakerloo Line / National Rail, Silverlink), North Wembley Station (Bakerloo Line / National Raid, Silverlink.

All offer easy access with short walking distances or by bus from outside the stations.
To plan your journey from your personal location, visit tfl.gov.uk

More detailed

Information on how to get to the practice will be emailed to you once your consultation appointment is booked.
If you require any further information, please do not hesitate to contact me.

Accessibility

The practice entrance is accessible via a short pathway. There are some steps outside the main entrance. Unfortunately, there is no wheelchair access.

There is no waiting area at the practice, so please arrive promptly at your appointment time.
